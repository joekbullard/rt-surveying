Rockell Tree Surveying

Arboriculture and Ecological Consultancy

George Rockell BSc (Hons), PTI, MArborA

George is the Director of Rockell Tree Surveying. George started his career in 2010 working as an arborist, climbing street trees in London. His dedication to trees and wildlife led to working as a Park Ranger for Waverley Borough Council and after succeeding in his Bachelor of Science in Land Based Management he started a role within an ecological consultancy company. After a few years working with bats, which included the publication of scientific research, George decided to pursue a career at the Wildlife Trust where he was the Countryside Surveyor responsible for the management of 38 reserves. As part of this role he was trusted in managing the tree safety inspections.
