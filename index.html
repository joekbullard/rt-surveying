Welcome to Rockell Tree Surveying


Rockell Tree Surveying provides specialist advice in the management of trees for both tree safety and bat roosting suitability. Based near Bristol, we serve clients across the south west and beyond. George Rockell (Director) has gained 10 years of experience across the arboricultural and ecological sectors - working as an arborist, in consultancy, for the Wildlife Trust and within local councils.


Please browse our site for information about the services we offer or get in touch if you want to discuss your requirements.
