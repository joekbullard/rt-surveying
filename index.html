Welcome to Rockell Tree Surveying

At Rockell Tree Surveying, we specialise in providing expert guidance for tree safety and bat roosting suitability. Located conveniently between Bristol and Bath, we extend our services to clients throughout the South West and beyond.

Dive into our website to explore the range of services we offer. Whether you're seeking tree care advice, assessing bat roosting potential, or need tailored solutions, we've got you covered.

Have specific requirements or questions? Don't hesitate to reach out to us. We're here to assist you in managing your trees, ensuring safety, and preserving the delicate balance of nature.
